Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Alias 2026 handles user information and protects your privacy when you use the application.

Developer: Herman Yurchanka
Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. General Information

Alias 2026 is a mobile game developed by Herman Yurchanka. This Privacy Policy describes what information the application collects, how it is used, and what rights users have in relation to their privacy.

2. Data Collection

Alias 2026 does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of users.

The application does not:
  • collect your name, email address, phone number, or location;
  • collect contacts, photos, or files from your device;
  • use analytics systems that identify you personally;
  • use tracking technologies for advertising purposes;
  • track your activity across third-party apps or websites.

Alias 2026 does not use tracking and does not build user profiles.

3. Local Data Storage

To ensure the normal operation of the game, Alias 2026 may store certain data locally on your device. This information is not sent to the developer and is not shared with third parties.

Locally stored data may include:

  • game settings;
  • game progress;
  • results and scores.

This data remains on your device and is used only to support the functionality of the application.

4. Subscription

Alias 2026 may offer access to premium features through a subscription.

All subscription payments are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. The developer does not receive or store your payment card details, banking information, or other payment credentials.

5. Subscription Management

Your subscription is managed by Apple through your Apple ID account.

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account upon confirmation of purchase.
  • You can manage your subscription in your Apple account settings.
  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through App Store settings.

Any billing matters, renewals, and cancellations are handled according to Apple’s subscription system and policies.

6. Third-Party Services

Alias 2026 does not use third-party analytics, advertising, marketing, or external data collection services.

Apart from Apple’s infrastructure required for app distribution and subscription processing, the application does not rely on external services that collect user data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Alias 2026 does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from users of any age.

Since no personal data is collected, the application is designed to be privacy-friendly for all users, including children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application or legal requirements.

If any updates are made, the revised version will be published on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
